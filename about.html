ABOUT US

Frank Ngbah's Foundation is a charity oriented foundation aimed at bringing back hope, care, quality education as well as quality healthcare to all manner of persons who needs it. Like we have already outlined, Education, Health and special needs assistance is our key areas of support along with regular aid to poor family.

We take this opportunity to extend our heartfelt thanks to all those who wish to assist us and also request for greater cooperation to achieve even more milestone within the charity sector. We welcome you to the site and look forward to ideas or suggestions that will help enhance the development of the charity sector within our beloved nation.

OUR MISSION

To design and implement effective social, emotional and psychological needs of children (orphans and the needy specailly), less previleged, aged in school and the communities at large inline with the UNs Sustainable Development Goals(SDGs 1234567).

OUR VISION

That all children, less privileged have a healthy sense of care, love, education and psychological wellbeing.
